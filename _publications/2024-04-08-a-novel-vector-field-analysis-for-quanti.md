> **TL;DR:** A novel vector field analysis method to quantitatively measure structural changes in the retina before and after macular epiretinal membrane surgery.

---

## What this paper is about

Epiretinal membrane (ERM) surgery aims to restore macular structure, but quantifying exactly how the retinal anatomy changes after surgery has been challenging. Traditional metrics like thickness maps miss the spatial deformation patterns that reveal how the retina recovers.

## Key idea

The authors propose a vector field analysis framework that captures and quantifies the directional structural changes in the macula following ERM surgery. This provides a richer, spatially-aware representation of how retinal layers shift and recover compared to simple scalar measurements.

## Why it matters

This gives ophthalmologists a more precise tool for evaluating surgical outcomes, potentially guiding post-operative care and helping predict visual recovery after ERM surgery.
